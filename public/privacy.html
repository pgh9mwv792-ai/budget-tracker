Privacy Policy

Budget Tracker · Last updated: July 4, 2026

Budget Tracker ("we", "us", the "app") is a personal finance application that helps you track spending, budget, and monitor your money and meals in one place. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used and protected, and the choices you have. We built this app to be private by default: we do not sell your data, share it for advertising, or show you ads.

The short version: Your financial and personal data is stored in your own secured account, visible only to you. We use trusted providers (Supabase, Plaid, Anthropic, Vercel) to run the app. Bank connections are read-only via Plaid. You can export or permanently delete all of your data at any time.

1. Who is responsible for your data

This app is operated by an independent developer. For any privacy questions or requests, contact us at cedarhale@icloud.com.

2. Information we collect

Account information

  • Your email address (used to sign in and to send confirmation, password-reset, and login-link emails).
  • An optional display name and profile picture, if you choose to add them.
  • Authentication data, including optional two-factor (TOTP) authentication settings.

Financial information

  • Transactions you enter manually, or that are imported when you connect a bank or card account through Plaid (date, amount, merchant/description, and category).
  • Budgets, savings goals, and category settings you create.
  • Receipt images you choose to scan. These are sent to our AI provider to extract the amount, date, and category, and are not stored by the app after processing.

Meal & nutrition information

  • Any foods, meals, macros, or nutrition targets you choose to log.

AI assistant information

  • Messages you send to the in-app assistant, and a summary of your app data (such as recent transactions and budgets) that is included so the assistant can answer accurately.
  • Optional "memories" — facts you explicitly ask the assistant to remember. These are stored privately in your account and can be viewed and deleted by you at any time.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide the app's core features: tracking, categorizing, budgeting, forecasting, and meal logging.
  • To import and update transactions from accounts you connect.
  • To answer your questions and perform actions you request through the AI assistant.
  • To secure your account and communicate essential service messages (e.g., email confirmation).

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or rent it to anyone.

4. Third-party services

The app relies on the following providers, each of which processes data only to make the app work:

  • Supabase — hosts our database and handles authentication. Your account and app data are stored here. See Supabase's privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.
  • Plaid — securely connects your bank/card accounts when you choose to link them. You enter your banking credentials directly with Plaid; we never see or store your bank login. Our access is read-only (we can view transactions, we cannot move money). See Plaid's end-user privacy policy.
  • Anthropic — powers the AI assistant and receipt scanning. The messages and app-data summary described above are sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses. Per Anthropic's API terms, this data is not used to train their models. See Anthropic's privacy policy.
  • Vercel — hosts the app's website. See Vercel's privacy policy.

5. How your data is stored and protected

  • Data is stored in a hosted PostgreSQL database with row-level security, so each account can only ever read or write its own records.
  • Access requires authentication, with optional two-factor authentication available.
  • All data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.
  • API keys and access tokens (including your Plaid access token) are stored server-side and are never exposed to your browser.

No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

6. Your choices and controls

  • Export your data: download a full copy of your data as a JSON file from Settings at any time.
  • Delete your account: permanently delete your account and all associated data from Settings. This cannot be undone.
  • Disconnect a bank: remove a linked Plaid connection at any time.
  • Manage AI memory: view and delete anything the assistant has remembered.

7. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your data is removed from our database. Please note that connected providers (such as Plaid) may retain records as required by their own policies and applicable law.

8. A note on sensitive information

Please avoid typing highly sensitive details (such as full card numbers, Social Security numbers, or passwords) into the AI assistant or notes fields. The assistant is designed for budgeting help and does not need this information.

9. Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

11. Contact

Questions or requests about your privacy? Email us at cedarhale@icloud.com.
